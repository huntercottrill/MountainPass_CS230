Hissho Sushi

About

Now open! Hissho Sushi offers an assortment of innovative rolls made fresh daily using only premium, responsibly sourced ingredients. The location replaced Mindful Café and includes a condensed York Street Market grab-and-go counter.

Not all menu items available at standard Hissho Sushi locations will be available at this location. To view menu favorites, see Hissho Sushi's full menu.

Hours

Monday-Friday: 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Saturday-Sunday: Closed

Accepted Payment Methods

Hissho Sushi will accept Dining Plan Swipes (from Prime 20, Prime 48, Prime 75 and Prime 190 plans), Dining Dollars, Mountie Bounty, credit/debit cards, and cash.

Location

64 Medical Center Drive

Morgantown, WV 26506

Located inside the Health Sciences Center.
